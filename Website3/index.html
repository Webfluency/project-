Information Technology Major

If you persue a BS in information technology (IT), you will develop a deep and wide understandding of a range of topics, issues, and applications that face leaderdship in today's technologically advancing world. You will develop a foundation of programming networking, and web-basedd competencies.

As an IT major, you then go on the specialize in business Intelligence, computer forensics, information architecture, or system administration, all of which provide real work examples and experience in IT problem-solving. You wil furher complement your knowledge to expand your undersatnding of important management or programming issues to further prepare you for your specific career path.
